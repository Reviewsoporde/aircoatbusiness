# **Briefing Web Designer — Airco@Business**

## **Project**

New website for Airco@Business as a pilot project.

* Delivery within 10 days after all input has been received

## **Website goal**

* Generate more quote requests from business clients  
* Convey trust and professionalism  
* Make clear why proper advice and choosing the right system matters  
* Offer fast, low-threshold ways to get in touch

## **Target audience**

Business decision-makers (offices, retail, hospitality, SMEs) dealing with:

* Staff being too hot or too cold  
* Poor climate control in offices  
* Reduced productivity due to an uncomfortable work climate  
* Existing systems that no longer run efficiently

## **Visual direction**

* **Primary color: black**  
* Strong contrast — everything must stay clearly readable  
* Premium, luxurious, professional look and feel  
* Align with the brand identity: company clothing and company vehicles as visual reference  
* Business-like, sleek, "high-end" feel — no consumer-style vibe

  make sure you use the logos like on the current website.

## **Key functionality**

* Clear, prominent CTAs aimed at requesting a quote  
* Separate button/redirect for consumers → links through to Airco at Home  
* Fast loading and mobile-friendly (business visitors often browse on the go)

## **Structure / page sections (indicative)**

1. Hero — strong business-oriented value proposition \+ direct CTA (request a quote)  
2. USPs: quality & service, reliability, solution-oriented and considerate technicians  
3. Services: advice, installation, maintenance  
4. Systems: cassette AC, ducted units, wall-mounted units — explaining which system fits which type of space  
5. Why a good climate pays off (figures/arguments — covered in the separate SEO/content brief)  
6. Projects/references: offices, a clothing store, and the example projects AVDT and Inventex (materials to follow)  
7. Service area: Hoofddorp – Rotterdam – Utrecht / all of South Holland (larger projects also possible beyond that)  
8. Contact / quote request form — emphasize speed of response  
9. Small, clear "residential customer?" button → redirect to Airco at Home

## **Materials provided by the client**

* Before/after photos of projects ( in the drive. only the after photos maybe make with ai the before photos.  
* Photos of offices, ceiling systems, installations  
* Team photos see the aircoatbusiness and airco at home website you can get alot of images from there.  
* Content/imagery from the client's social media channels, for consistent branding

## **Note**

* The design must clearly stand apart from the consumer brand (Airco at Home): more business-oriented, more premium, less "warm/approachable," more "expert/high-end"
